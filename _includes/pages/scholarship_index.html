Student Scholarship

As part of RubyConfIndia's commitment to encourage students in India to excel in computing and technology, we are offering student scholarships (selected few, based on some criteria, as explained below).

Student Scholarship Conditions
  • The student must be studying in a School / College / University in India at the time of the conference (Jan. 2017). They must show proof of being a student, before the conference begins.
  • They will learn the Ruby programming language and solve an assignment within a given time period.
  • A committee will evaluate the student's code and short-list the candidates eligible for this scholarship.
  • Attend the core day(s) of the main conference i.e. 28, 29 Jan. 2017.
  • The selected scholars will give a brief presentation of about 5 minutes during the lightning talks at the conference.
  • The last date to submit your application is 14th Nov. 2016.
What will the student get as part of the scholarship?
  • A free conference pass for the 2 day conference.
  • Travel (by III tier rail) and accommodation costs. These will be paid after the conference.
